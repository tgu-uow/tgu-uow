About
TJPU-UOW Lab. provides open develop environment for students who with a strong interest in computer vision and robotics, the opportunity to expose to a breadth of knowledge in the field with the goal of leveraging AI for social good. Students engage with faculty, staff and researchers who works in the School of Electronics and Information Engineering.



Curriculum
The program curriculum includes the following:

Self-aid Learning

Machine Learning Lectures Python & C++ programing language Baidu PaddlePaddle

Hands-on Experience

Academic/discipline competitions Ongoing research projects from enterprise Discussion on cutting-edge technology on AI

Personal Growth

Discussions and presentations with peers Small group mentoring by experts and graduate students Presentation opportunity in International Conference Talk with foreigner experts and international students
